# Purpose

The Constitution is referenced throughout the Pamora Foundation (AI Master Context, Product Philosophy, PRD template) as the second document in the source-of-truth hierarchy, immediately after the AI Master Context and before the Engineering Playbook. It is intended to hold the non-negotiable rules that bind every other document — the rules a PRD, an ADR, or an implementation may never override.

---

# Current State

Not yet authored as a standalone document. Until it is written, its role is partially covered by:

* [AI Master Context](../00-overview/00-ai-master-context.md) — Non-Negotiable Rules section.
* [Product Philosophy](../00-overview/03-product-philosophy.md) — the 12 guiding principles.
* [Engineering Playbook](02-engineering-playbook.md) — "Things We Do Not Do" section.

These should be consolidated into a formal Constitution before the platform scales past MVP.
