# Value Proposition

**Version:** 1.0.0

**Status:** Approved

---

# Purpose

This document states, in a single place, the value Pamora delivers to each side of the platform.

It is derived from the Business Model and Competition Analysis, and should be used as the reference for messaging, onboarding copy, and product prioritization.

---

# Positioning Statement

Pamora is **not an Event Management App**.

Pamora is an **Occasion Collaboration Platform with an Integrated Marketplace**.

This positioning differentiates Pamora from ticketing platforms, conference software, invitation generators, vendor directories, and generic productivity applications.

---

# Proposed Tagline

> **Plan Together. Fund Together. Celebrate Together.**

This directly reflects the three things that differentiate Pamora:

* **Plan** → Occasion Workspace
* **Fund** → Contributions & Harambee
* **Celebrate** → Vendors, Execution & Memories

---

# Value by Persona

## For Occasion Hosts

* Structured planning
* Committee collaboration
* Budget visibility
* Contribution tracking
* Vendor discovery
* Booking management
* Historical records

The Host gains confidence and saves time.

---

## For Committee Members

* Shared responsibilities
* Task management
* Clear communication
* Visibility into progress

The committee becomes more coordinated.

---

## For Guests

* Digital invitations
* Easy RSVP
* Contribution options
* Announcements
* Occasion updates

Participation becomes simpler.

---

## For Vendors

* Business visibility
* Qualified customers
* Booking management
* Availability calendar
* Reputation through verified reviews

Pamora becomes a customer acquisition channel.
